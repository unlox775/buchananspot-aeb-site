Hans Sorensen
Born: 2 October 1825, Kragevig, Ørslev, Holbæk, Denmark
Christened: 3 October 1825 (at home), 6 November 1825 (in Church), Ørslev, Holbæk, Denmark
Vaccinated (Smallpox): 7 September 1826
Married Ane Nielsdatter 9 September 1848, Tersløse, Holbæk, Denmark
Married Matilda (Evensen) Torgersen 3 June 1880, Salt Lake City, Salt Lake, Utah, USA
Died: 6 April 1893, Aurora, Sevier, Utah, USA
