 October 10, 2007

Started with film 52316 pg 35 to re-find Jens Svendsen birth record. Then continuing found:
Pg. 36 Eegerup 3 oct 1687 Kirsten daughter of Oluf Jenssen (already found)
Pg. 37 Ørslev 14 Oct 1688 Karen daughter of Svend Jenssen and Inger NEW
pg. 38 Egerup 7 Jun 1691 Dorthe, daughter of Oluf Jensen - already found
pg. 38 Ørslev Nativitatis 1: Christi 1691 Peder, son of Anders Svendssen
pg. 39 Ørslev 2 Mar 1694 Jens, son of Anders Svendssen
perhaps from this point, Svend Jenssen's family is no longer listed in this parish. Check the Gyrstinge film for more children

film 0052333
nothing found for children of Svend Jenssen and Inger or Ingeborg between 1688 and 1701
cursory search for death of children in the same time period found nothing of note. There was a woman, 36 years old with the last name of Svendsdaater who died in this period
deaths end in 1769 then marriages from 1645
no marriage of Oluf Jensen and Sidsel Knudsdatter found in 1690 here either

back to 0052316 Egerup
death records -
pg 94 found Oluf, son of Oluf Jensen died 23 July 1682 Egerup 5/4 years old
p 99 found death of Anders Svendsen in Bringstrup, age 42
p 100 death (burial) of Peder, Oluf Jensen's son 16 p Trini 1700 5 weeks (Ügre) old Egerup (date is 26 sep)
p 101 death (burial) of Povel, Oluf Jensen's son 9 Oct 1701 age 6 days Egerup
p 103 death (burial) of Oluf Jensen, Egerup, 6 aug 1708 age: 66

Went back into birth records p. 31 and found Oluf son of Oluf Jensen and Kirsten, christened 4 Aug (10 p Trini) 1678
p 46 christening of Peder, son of Oluf Jensen in Egerup 19 Sept 1700
p 47 christening of Povel, son of Oluf Jensen in Egerup 2 Oct 1701
Looked for marriage record of Oluf Jensen and Kirsten between 1666 and 1678 and found nothing.

October 11
After studying the probate record and comparing it with other records, I concluded that the Svend Jensen married to Inger Sorensen and Karen Peitersdr all from Assentorp is not our family. I removed them from my PAF.

Checked IGI and most of this has no temple work.
