Research Plans

I have had some moderate success in finding new information on some of the family lines. I intend to continue this. There are a couple of microfilms that cover the area of the family in Sorø and as I get time to go to the library, I will continue to work on it. The nature of the information is inexact and can get confusing. Sometimes I just have to make my best guess. I pray for guidance and so far I feel I have been led to where I have found records that make sense.
  • Continue Svendsen research, including sibling lines.
  • Research some of the other end lines in original Danish records.
