Research Processes

My main effort lately was in early Kentucky records. Some work also has been done to try to find any further records in Ireland.

  • Details of research 2006 - Trip to Kentucky, then research of Kentucky records in Salt Lake.

Research Plans

I have been working on my theory about some tie in with the name Waller Overton and the man by that name who lived in Kentucky at the same time Archibald Waller Overton Buchanan was born. I started researching the old Kentucky newspapers that began in the late 1700's. That is slow work, but may yield some information about the Back/Bach and Buchanan lines. Vital records from the time are sketchy.
  • Continue research of old Kentucky Newspapers
  • Do some research into the brothers and sisters, aunt and uncles of AWO Buchanan. There is a lot of missing data.
  • Continue to study the possibility of getting Irish records that may fill in information about the family as they lived there.
